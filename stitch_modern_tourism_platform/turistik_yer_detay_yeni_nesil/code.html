About this experience

Embark on a captivating journey through the legendary vermilion gates of Fushimi Inari Taisha, escaping the daytime crowds to experience the mountain's true mystique. This exclusive twilight tour offers a profound exploration of Japan's most important shrine dedicated to Inari, the Shinto god of rice and commerce.

As the sun dips below the horizon, the winding trails illuminate softly, casting ethereal shadows among the thousands of torii gates. Your expert local guide will share centuries of lore, uncovering the hidden meanings behind the fox statues and the deep-rooted spiritual practices that continue to thrive on this sacred mountain.
